A5 - Demo of an AI application that entails two different instances of AI tasks
This system uses a two-stage AI pipeline to generate personalized fitness plans.
Task 1 (Classification): A Random Forest model predicts the optimal workout structure (e.g., Full Body, Upper/Lower, Push/Pull/Legs, Body Part) based on user constraints.
Task 2 (Generation): A Large Language Model (Gemini 2.5) synthesizes the final weekly schedule, tailoring volume and exercise selection to the user's age, gender, and goals.
AI Transparency: LIME is employed to explain the Random Forest's predictions, highlighting key factors influencing the workout structure choice.
